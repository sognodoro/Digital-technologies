Evolution of digital devices

The Internet

The internet was originally a government project developed by the US defense department, to communicate and share resources on the intranet. However this was localised. IP protocols were established so that different networks could communicate, this lead to the developemnet of email. The introduction of browsers gave the public an unlimited digital repository of information Eventually with the improvement of delivery systems, routers, optic cable, Improved mobile connectivity surpassing PC traffic by 2010, The internet of things, The internet has grown to immeasurable porportions.

Mobile Phones

In 1973 mobile telephony was limited to vehicles. The first comercially available mobile phone was developed by E.F Johnson ans Millicom Inc. However in 1973 Motorola made the first mobile phone that was not linited on to vehicles, It weighed 2kg and measured 23 * 13 cm. The introduction of 1G helped drive the mass market use of Mobiles, howevwe it had major security flaws. With 2G, came text messaging and improved security. With more devices on the internet it becama apparent that 2G would not cope, so 3G was developed with improved speed and coverage. 3G could not cope with streaming services, so 4G was implemented to solve this issue. 5G the cureent standard employs increase speed, coverage and security.

Computers

The first mechanical computer was developed by Charles Babbage. This lead to the creation of analog computers. In 1937 Claude Shannon laid the foundations of modern computing by the application of Boolean algebra and the swithing of circuits. With the introduction of vacuum tubes and electronic circuits, computer became more adept at processing. Colossus was the worlds' first electronic digital programmable computer. Transistors and integrated circuits completely transformed computing and gave rise to the very powerful mobile computers and desktops we have today.
